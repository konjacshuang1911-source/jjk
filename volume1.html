Summary

Jujutsu Kaisen Volume 1 introduces Yuji Itadori, a kind-hearted but unusually strong high schooler whose life changes when he swallows a cursed object—one of Ryomen Sukuna’s fingers—and becomes host to the most dangerous curse in existence.
