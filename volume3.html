Summary

Volume 3 covers the buildup to the Kyoto Goodwill Event, early clashes between Tokyo and Kyoto students, and a disturbing case involving a cursed spirit causing grotesque body alterations. It focuses on character development for Fushiguro, Kugisaki, and Yuji while setting up major conflicts for the next arc.
