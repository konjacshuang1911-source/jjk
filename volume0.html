Summary

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 follows Yuta Okkotsu, a shy teenager haunted by the powerful curse of his childhood friend Rika Orimoto, who died in an accident but now violently protects him. He enrolls at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where Satoru Gojo trains him to control his cursed energy and help others. As Yuta grows stronger and forms bonds with his classmates, he becomes a target of the dangerous sorcerer Suguru Geto, who seeks to use Rika’s immense power for his own plans. In the end, Yuta confronts Geto, accepts his connection with Rika, and releases her spirit, gaining confidence and choosing to continue life as a jujutsu sorcerer.
