Summary

Jujutsu Kaisen Volume 5 focuses on the climax of the Kyoto Goodwill Event as Yuji Itadori and Aoi Todo team up against the special grade curse Hanami. During the intense battle, Yuji improves his control of cursed energy and lands multiple powerful Black Flashes, while Todo supports him with his unique ability and strategic thinking. Meanwhile, other students and teachers fight to protect the school from additional curse users working with Mahito and Suguru Geto. The volume highlights teamwork, growth, and the increasing danger posed by the villains, ending with the event disrupted and the threat of stronger enemies becoming more serious.
